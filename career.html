Career

2017 – present

Associate Professor with Habilitation, Atlântica - Instituto Universtário, lecturing Glass and Ceramics, Metallic Materials, Advanced Aerospace Materials, Production Processes, and Engineering Project.

1

2002 – present

Research Member of the Research Unity nº 151 C-MAST (Centre for Mechanical and Aerospace Science and Technologies – Portuguese Science and Technology Foundation - FCT).

2

2001 – 2016

Director of the PhD programme in Industrial Engineering and Management, University of Beira Interior, Covilhã, Portugal, from September 2001 until December 2016.

3

1992 – 2016

Associate Professor with Habilitation of the Department of Electromechanical Engineering, University of Beira Interior (UBI), Covilhã, Portugal, where he lectured Materials Science and Engineering, New Materials, Technological Forecasting, and Innovation and Technology Management for undergraduate and graduate engineering students.

4

1990 – 1992

Senior researcher and lecturer at Polytechnic Institute of Rio de Janeiro, Brazil.

5

1988 – 1990

Technical Director, Hellas S.A. Trade and Industry, enterprise for the trade and development of alumina based advanced ceramics, São José dos Campos, Brazil.

6

1987 – 1990

Director, Quantum Materials Engineering Consultants, São José dos Campos, São Paulo, Brazil.

7

1983 – 1990

Senior researcher and lecturer at Aerospace Technical Center (CTA), Materials Division (PMR), São José dos Campos, São Paulo, Brazil.

8

1982 – 1983

Associate Professor, Army Engineering Institute (IME), Rio de Janeiro, Brazil. (teaching Ceramic Materials).

9

1976 – 1981

Phd Student at the Institut für Werkstoffwiss enschaften der Friedrich-Alexander Universität Erlangen-Nürnberg, Erlangen, Germany.

10

1974 – 1976

Assistant Professor, Army Engineering Institute (IME), Rio de Janeiro, Brazil. (teaching Introduction to Materials Science).

11

1971 – 1974

Assistant Professor, Physics Institute of the Fluminense Federal University (UFF), Niteroi, Brazil (teaching Mechanics and Quantum Mechanics).

12

1970 – 1974

Assistant Professor, Physics Institute of the University of Estado do Rio de Janeiro (UERJ), Rio de Janeiro, Brazil (teaching Quantum Mechanics and Theory of Relativity).
